Bolik University's History

In 1967, Bolik University was officially established as a college specifically designed for higher vocational education and the field of computer science. In 1990, the school was officially rebuilt as a university. This is a modern comprehensive university directly under the jurisdiction of the federal government. With excellent teaching and strong teaching staff, it enjoys a high reputation in the local area.
